eBay Design



An amazing 12 weeks of learning about e-commerce, the online fashion market, in the midst of the introduction of one of the biggest initiatives in buyer experience at eBay.

{/* */}

For 12 weeks of the summer of 2018, I interned at eBay in San Jose, California. Over the course of my internship in the Buyer Experience team, I worked on designing an extension to eBay's product based commerce experience into the fashion vertical. I worked with both the fashion and shopping experience teams to bring a near-term vision to life with wireframe flows, detailed hi-res mocks, and clickthrough prototypes. In addition, I also worked closely with PMs on a feature to improve the experience of browsing and comparing listings across the site, using wireframes of low to high fidelity to communicate and iterate on ideas.
